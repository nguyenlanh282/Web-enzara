## 2. Brand Identity

### Visual Identity
**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Earth green (#626c13, #738136) - dominant
- Secondary: Orange (#de8d1e, #e67e22)
- Accent: Yellow (#ffcc48)
- Supporting: White, natural tones

**Typography:** SVN-Gilroy family (bold, semi-bold, regular)

**Design Language:**
- Clean, modern e-commerce aesthetic
- Generous white space
- Rounded corners (10-20px border-radius)
- Soft shadows
- Natural imagery (pineapple fields, fresh produce)
- Scientific diagrams (enzyme structures)

### Brand Positioning
**Tagline:** "Sức mạnh của sự chuyển hóa Enzyme Dứa" (Power of Pineapple Enzyme Transformation)

**Core Values:**
- Eco-consciousness & sustainability
- Scientific transparency
- Community impact
- Chemical-free safety
- Local farmer support

**Tone of Voice:**
- Educational & informative
- Mission-driven
- Community-focused
- Trustworthy & transparent

## 5. Notable Features & USPs

### Unique Selling Points
1. **Enzyme Technology** - Bromelain, Peroxidase, Cellulase, Amylase from pineapple waste
2. **90-Day Fermentation Process** - transparent production story
3. **Zero Chemical Harmful Residue** - safe for children/sensitive skin
4. **Upcycling Model** - converts pineapple waste into cleaning products
5. **Social Impact** - supports Gia Lai pineapple farmers with stable income

### Trust Signals
- Ministry of Industry & Trade certification
- Laboratory testing certificates (dedicated page)
- Detailed ingredient transparency
- Factory information disclosure
- 5-star customer reviews
- Q&A sections on products

### Service Features
- **Free COD** nationwide shipping
- Free returns/exchanges for defective items
- Member discount programs
- Combo deals & promotions
- Customer support: 0945.139.990

### Conversion Tactics
- Prominent discount badges (24-33% off)
- "New" product tags
- Sticky add-to-cart bar
- Related product recommendations
- Upsell combos/gifts
- Customer Q&A for social proof

## 6. Content Strategy

- Heavy emphasis on enzyme science education

- Blog content about safety & cleaning
- Transparent ingredient sourcing
- Production process storytelling

### Storytelling Elements
- Origin story tied to pineapple waste upcycling
- Community impact narrative (farmer support)
- Environmental sustainability messaging
- Factory transparency

## 7. Competitive Positioning

### Market Differentiation
- **Not just eco-friendly** - scientifically backed enzyme technology
- **Local production** - Vietnamese-made with local sourcing
- **Social enterprise angle** - farmer support program
- **Premium natural** - positioned above conventional cleaners, below luxury imports

### Barriers to Entry
- 90-day fermentation proprietary process
- Government certifications
- Established farmer relationships
- Brand trust in natural cleaning space
